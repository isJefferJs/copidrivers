Delete Your Copi Account

Last updated: March 15, 2026

This page explains how you can request deletion of your Copi account and the personal data associated with it.

To request account deletion, email us at copidrivers@gmail.com using the subject line: Copi Account Deletion Request

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to copidrivers@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line Copi Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email address associated with your Copi account.
  4. If your Copi account uses Google Sign-In, please mention that in your email so we can identify your account correctly.

What will be deleted

Once your request is verified and processed, we will delete or anonymize the personal data associated with your Copi account that is no longer necessary to retain.

  • Your Copi account record.
  • Your authentication record associated with Copi.
  • Your app profile data linked to your account.
  • Other personal data stored by Copi that is directly associated with your account, unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or compliance reasons.

What may be retained

Some limited data may be retained after account deletion where necessary for legal compliance, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, enforcement of our terms, or security purposes.

  • Security and diagnostic logs.
  • Records needed to investigate abuse, fraud, or misuse of the service.
  • Records required to comply with applicable legal obligations.

Retention periods

We aim to process account deletion requests within 30 days of verifying the request.

Data that must be retained for security, fraud-prevention, or legal reasons may be kept for up to 90 days after deletion request processing, or longer if required by law or ongoing legal obligations.

Subscriptions and Google Play purchases

Deleting your Copi account does not automatically cancel an active subscription purchased through Google Play. Subscription management and purchase records handled by Google Play are subject to Google’s own systems and policies.

If you have an active subscription, you should also manage or cancel it directly through your Google Play account.

Additional information

If we need more information to verify your identity or locate your account, we may contact you using the email address from which you sent the request.

Contact

If you have questions about account deletion or your data, contact: copidrivers@gmail.com
